Paradox Collection

A personal list of curious psychological phenomena, paradoxes and cognitive biases

So, this is just a small personal project that came to being because of an interest on random things and because I wanted to put to practice my recently acquired basic knowledge on HTML, CSS and JavaScript. That said, I know there's hundreds of paradoxes and phenomenons like the ones included on this page, but if I wanted to include them all it would just be a thorough exhaustive list without cause or meaning. Instead, I detailed only the ones that I personally had fun finding out with, that have a specific usefulness for me or that woke my curiosity for whatever reason. Some of them actually let me view personal situations with a new lens or perspective.

However, I would also like for this list to be collaborative and I'm always interesting in curious facts in general, so if you can think of a phenomenon that really interests you or that you somehow applied to your day-to-day life please let me know about it!.
